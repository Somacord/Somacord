# Somacord SEO Strategy

## Local SEO Focus
Target long-tail, intent-driven local queries, not generic brand terms.

Priority phrases (replicate per city):
- make friends in [city]
- social groups in [city]
- things to do in [city]
- adult friendship groups [city]
- meet new people in [city]
- coffee meetups [city]
- hiking groups [city]

## City Landing Pages
Pattern: /cities/[city]

Each city page should:
- Target "[activity] in [city]" and "meet people in [city]" queries
- Show example gatherings and local activity categories (clearly labeled as examples pre-launch)
- Include a Speed Connect CTA above the fold
- Link to Community Partners in that city as they onboard

## Content Principles
- No fabricated statistics or member counts for SEO copy.
- Prioritize real, specific local detail (neighborhoods, activity types) over generic filler.
- Titles/meta should lead with the human outcome ("Find your people in Salt Lake City") not the mechanism ("Social networking platform").
