# Somacord User Journey

**Status:** Consumer journey rewritten around the approved growth flywheel — supersedes the earlier "join membership first" version. See [/docs/business/business-model.md](../business/business-model.md#revenue-priority) and [/docs/business/launch-strategy.md](../business/launch-strategy.md).

## Growth Flywheel (Core Journey)

Membership is not the front door — a Community Partner event is. Meetaway/Speed Connect is the acquisition and connection engine that moves someone from a single event into an ongoing member; it is not the product being sold. See [/docs/brand/experience-language.md](../brand/experience-language.md).

1. Discover a Community Partner event
2. Create a free Somacord account
3. Experience Meetaway Speed Connect
4. Attend Community Gatherings
5. Become a paid Member
6. Attend Official Somacord Events
7. Invite friends
8. More members → attracts more Community Partners

Each loop back to step 8 is what makes this a flywheel, not a funnel: more members make Somacord more attractive to new Community Partners (bigger audience to reach), and more Community Partners create more entry points at step 1. See [/docs/business/business-model.md](../business/business-model.md) for how this maps to revenue.

## Community Partner Journey

Organizations are not members and don't go through the flywheel above as a customer — they're the entry point that feeds it. See [/docs/business/community-partners.md](../business/community-partners.md#organizations-not-members).

Organization (already runs a coffee shop, brewery, restaurant, or community group)
→ Inquire / apply as a Community Partner (concierge-run at launch — see [launch-strategy.md](../business/launch-strategy.md#launch-sequence-partner-events-first-not-memberships))
→ Choose a partner product: One-Time or Ongoing (see [community-partners.md](../business/community-partners.md#partner-products))
→ Create an organization profile
→ Add Partner Events
→ Introduce their existing community to Somacord
→ Grow local presence and reach new people

There is no shared membership subscription — Community Partners pay through partner products (flat fee, revenue share, or recurring subscription, per event), not the Somacord Membership. See [/docs/business/pricing.md](../business/pricing.md#community-partner-pricing-b2b).

See [/docs/business/membership.md](../business/membership.md) and [/docs/business/community-partners.md](../business/community-partners.md) for the business-side detail behind this journey.
