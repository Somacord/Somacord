# Somacord MVP Requirements

## Included in MVP

**Public website**
- Homepage (hero, featured gatherings, city selection, how it works, membership preview, community partners section)
- Gatherings discovery page (community and partner experiences, clearly labeled examples)
- Gathering detail page
- Speed Connect page (what it is, what to expect, why it's low pressure)
- Membership page (Somacord Membership plans, example month, join CTA)
- Community Partners page (recruiting local community builders)
- City page (Salt Lake City at launch)

**Account flow**
- Signup
- Profile setup (name, city, interests, activities)
- Member home (this week, Speed Connect booking)
- Member profile
- Create gathering

**Mobile app (preview only for MVP)**
- Signup
- Member home
- Gathering discovery
- Gathering detail
- Profile

## Not Included in MVP
- Admin dashboards
- Help center / safety center
- In-app messaging (beyond gathering RSVP)
- Sponsored placement or paid partner tiers
- Fake testimonials, fake events, fake partner logos, or fake member counts
- Multiple city launches (Salt Lake City only at launch)

## Launch Honesty Rule
Until real gatherings and members exist in a city, the site must clearly label content as "example" and never imply an active community that does not yet exist.

See [features.md](features.md) for the same MVP scope organized as a feature list, and [roadmap.md](roadmap.md) for what comes after.
