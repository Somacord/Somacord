# Somacord Experience Language

Naming and terminology rules for consistent product, marketing, and engineering language.

## Customer-Facing Terms
- **"Speed Connect"** — the guided-conversation experience. This is the only customer-facing name; it is never referred to by its underlying vendor/technology name (Meetaway). Speed Connect is **not** the primary product — it's the acquisition and connection engine that leads into real-world gatherings and events. See [/docs/business/business-model.md](../business/business-model.md#meetaway-access): free users get limited sessions (lead generation), members get unlimited sessions.
- **"Gatherings"** — the general term for community and partner experiences/events. Prefer "gatherings" over "events" in customer-facing copy.
- **"Somacord Membership"** — the member subscription name, launch target $29/month (see [/docs/business/pricing.md](../business/pricing.md#consumer-pricing-b2c)). Do not use this name for Community Partner pricing — partners have entirely separate products.
- **"Community Partners"** — organizations (coffee shops, restaurants, clubs, hobby groups, event organizers), not members. See [/docs/business/community-partners.md](../business/community-partners.md).

## Internal-Only Terms
- **Meetaway** — the third-party virtual conversation technology provider powering Speed Connect. Internal/vendor use only. Never surfaced in customer-facing product, marketing, or support copy. Its business role is customer acquisition and a membership benefit, not the product Somacord sells — see [/docs/product/user-journey.md](../product/user-journey.md#growth-flywheel-core-journey).

## Principle
Somacord is a "friendship-first social club," not a dating app, social feed, or networking tool — copy and UI language should avoid dating-app or professional-networking conventions (e.g. no "matches," no swiping language, no appearance-based framing).
