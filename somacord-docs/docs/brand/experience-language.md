# Somacord Experience Language

Naming and terminology rules for consistent product, marketing, and engineering language.

## Customer-Facing Terms
- **"Speed Connect"** — the guided-conversation experience. This is the only customer-facing name; it is never referred to by its underlying vendor/technology name.
- **"Gatherings"** — the general term for community and partner experiences/events. Prefer "gatherings" over "events" in customer-facing copy.
- **"Somacord Membership"** — the membership plan name, available Monthly ($39), Quarterly ($99), or Yearly ($349).
- **"Community Partners"** — coffee shops, restaurants, clubs, hobby groups, and event organizers who join Somacord to organize gatherings for their existing community.

## Internal-Only Terms
- **Meetaway** — the third-party virtual conversation technology provider powering Speed Connect. Internal/vendor use only. Never surfaced in customer-facing product, marketing, or support copy.

## Principle
Somacord is a "friendship-first social club," not a dating app, social feed, or networking tool — copy and UI language should avoid dating-app or professional-networking conventions (e.g. no "matches," no swiping language, no appearance-based framing).
