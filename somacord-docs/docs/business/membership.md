# Somacord Membership

Member (consumer) subscriptions only. For Community Partner pricing, see [pricing.md](pricing.md#community-partner-pricing-b2b) and [community-partners.md](community-partners.md) — Community Partners are organizations and no longer purchase this subscription (see [business-model.md](business-model.md#superseded)).

**Membership is not how someone enters Somacord** — a Community Partner event is. See [/docs/product/user-journey.md](../product/user-journey.md#growth-flywheel-core-journey) for the full flywheel; membership is step 5 of 8, reached after someone has already attended a real event and tried Meetaway Speed Connect for free.

## Model
Two consumer tiers — see [pricing.md](pricing.md#consumer-pricing-b2c) for full detail:

- **Free** — browse public gatherings/events, limited RSVPs, limited Meetaway Speed Connect access (lead generation), public profile, discover local community. No subscription.
- **Member Subscription** — launch target **$29/month**. Unlimited RSVPs, **unlimited** Meetaway Speed Connect access, early registration, discounts on Official Somacord Events, member-only experiences. An annual plan is architecturally prepared but not confirmed for MVP launch.

Membership deepens engagement — it does not gate discovery. Anyone (Free tier or signed-out) can browse gatherings, try Speed Connect for free (rate-limited), or buy a one-time ticket to an Official Somacord Event without a subscription. See [pricing.md](pricing.md) and [business-model.md](business-model.md#meetaway-access).

## Member Journey
See [/docs/product/user-journey.md](../product/user-journey.md#growth-flywheel-core-journey) for the authoritative journey — membership is reached partway through the flywheel (Partner event → free account → Meetaway → Community Gatherings → **Member Subscription** → Official Somacord Events → invite friends), not the entry point.

**Status:** Membership pricing and inclusions above are approved as launch targets. Member discounts on Partner/Somacord events are directional (per [business-model.md](business-model.md)'s Experience Economy strategy) — exact discount mechanics are not yet specified. The Free tier's "limited RSVPs" and "limited Speed Connect sessions" restrictions have no enforcement anywhere in the product yet — see [/docs/engineering/database-schema.md](../engineering/database-schema.md#known-gaps-vs-business-model-v2).
