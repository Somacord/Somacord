# Somacord Membership

Member (consumer) subscriptions only. For Community Partner pricing, see [pricing.md](pricing.md#community-partner-pricing) and [community-partners.md](community-partners.md) — Community Partners are organizations and no longer purchase this subscription (see [business-model.md](business-model.md#superseded)).

## Model
One membership — the "Somacord Membership" — available Monthly ($39), Quarterly ($99), or Yearly ($349). Same benefits on every plan; billing frequency is the only difference between them.

Includes:
- Speed Connect access, ongoing
- Community access and member discovery
- Ability to create and RSVP to gatherings
- Member discounts and early registration on select Partner and Somacord events
- Community access

Membership deepens engagement — it does not gate discovery. Anyone can browse gatherings, try Speed Connect for free, or buy a one-time ticket to a Partner or Somacord event without joining. See [pricing.md](pricing.md).

## Member Journey
Visitor → Discover Somacord → Join Free Speed Connect → Experience first connection → Become Member → Discover gatherings → Join community → Create gatherings → Invite others.

Full journey detail: [/docs/product/user-journey.md](../product/user-journey.md).

**Status:** Membership pricing and inclusions above are approved. Member discounts on Partner/Somacord events are directional (per [business-model.md](business-model.md)'s Experience Economy strategy) — exact discount mechanics are not yet specified.
