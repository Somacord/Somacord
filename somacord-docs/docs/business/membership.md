# Somacord Membership

Member (consumer) subscriptions only. For Community Partner pricing, see [pricing.md](pricing.md#community-partner-pricing-b2b) and [community-partners.md](community-partners.md) — Community Partners are organizations and no longer purchase this subscription (see [business-model.md](business-model.md#superseded)).

## Model
Two consumer tiers — see [pricing.md](pricing.md#consumer-pricing-b2c) for full detail:

- **Free** — browse public gatherings/events, limited RSVPs, public profile, discover local community. No subscription.
- **Member Subscription** — launch target **$29/month**. Unlimited RSVPs, full Speed Connect access, early registration, discounts on Official Somacord Events, member-only experiences. Quarterly/annual plans may be added later; not part of MVP launch.

Membership deepens engagement — it does not gate discovery. Anyone (Free tier or signed-out) can browse gatherings, try Speed Connect for free, or buy a one-time ticket to an Official Somacord Event without a subscription. See [pricing.md](pricing.md).

## Member Journey
Visitor → Discover Somacord → Join Free Speed Connect → Experience first connection → Become Member → Discover gatherings → Join community → Create gatherings → Invite others.

Full journey detail: [/docs/product/user-journey.md](../product/user-journey.md).

**Status:** Membership pricing and inclusions above are approved as launch targets. Member discounts on Partner/Somacord events are directional (per [business-model.md](business-model.md)'s Experience Economy strategy) — exact discount mechanics are not yet specified. The Free tier's "limited RSVPs" restriction has no enforcement anywhere in the product yet — see [/docs/engineering/database-schema.md](../engineering/database-schema.md#known-gaps-vs-business-model-v2).
